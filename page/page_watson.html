Introduction
About Watson Adventures

Watson Adventures is a company that provides scavanger hunt events. The company was founded in 1999, and around 500,000 participants across the US. It was designed for general public of all ages as well as corporate groups for team building. They provide fun ways to explore museums, famous public spaces (parks, monuments), and neighbourhoods.

Types of events
Given Scope:

Make hybrid hunts a better experience for both hosts and participants



Why this challenge?

Good for business: can have presence in more cities and have bigger market It is a more affordable option:


“We won't have to pay our staff additionally for reaching the remote location. If a client has a tight budget, we can arrange for a remote host.”

What are the recognized problems (by them)?
  1. Zoom setup
  2. Zoom via their phone and check their game 'app' (on a browser) at the same time
  3. Uses a high amount of phone battery
  4. Disturbance: No quiet environment No stable internet connection
  5. Browser-based game: how to make “a browser to kind of act like an app” (-Stacy)
  6. Tight on budget

“The more complicated we make the app the more bells and whistles we keep building into it. The chances of it breaking get higher and higher.” … “we already have a lot of challenges getting it to operate smoothly on Apple and Android”

Solutions

  1. No promote about event on social media
    2. Solution: Social Media - posts that promote upcoming events




  2. Unclear information about hunt during the booking process.
    3. Solution: Add program explanation on the booking process pages




  3. Some participants don't read the booking mail carefully
    4. Solution: Add different options for getting information update when booking




  4. Information hierarchy and structure unclear
    5. Solution: Enhance the instructions graphically




  5. Game Route
    6. Room for Improvement: clear and linear game route, from start to end point.




  6. Hard to find restroom and water during the hunt
    7. Solution: Add icons when the clue is close to the rest spot




  7. Unclear question navigation process
    8. Solution: Combine location and photo questions




  8. Currently the process of uploading photo is complex
    9. Solution: Simplify & clarify the process of uploading photos




  9. No visual indication of alert
    10. Solution: Make alert for announcements




  10. Current alarm is for every team
    11. Solution: Host send message to specific group




  11. The way to end the game is not clear.
    12. Solution: Add one more step to clearly explain.




  12. Photo gallery
    13. Room for Improvement: Images that can be edited and shared




  13. Lack of incentive for next hunt
    14. Solution: Incentive for second time customers
