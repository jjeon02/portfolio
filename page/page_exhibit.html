Cuckooland: Fantasy Market Exhibition

Exhibition

2021

Cuckooland is a conceptual market that sells abstract concepts. It's a complex, symbiotic and parasitic space that is a reflection of how society functions. The things it sold were abstract concepts that exist but cannot be purchased.
