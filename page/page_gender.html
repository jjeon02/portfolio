Gender and Robot

Editorial Design, Typography, Illustration

2022

"The Definition of Robot and Gender" is a project that questions the boundaries between genders and how it affects the way a person perceives a robot: a technology that is expected to interact most with people or even perceived as one. This booklet will let the reader question their pre-existing bias on gender, robot, and the relationship between those two topics.
