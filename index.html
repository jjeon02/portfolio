Jueun Jeon is a designer from S.Korea who went to School of Visual Arts for Interaction Design MFA (STEM) and Rhode Island School of Design for Graphic Design BFA. Jueun worked for RISD Global (Editorial Design), pxd(GUI Design), Art & Design International (Web Design), and Sir.Loin(GUI Design). Jueun also worked with Bigtincan, Watson Adventures for SVA's class.
